# Sovereign Cloud Overview

## Introduction

Cloud sovereignty is the principle that organizations and nations must maintain control over their data, operations, and digital infrastructure. As cloud adoption accelerates globally, sovereignty has become a critical architectural driver — especially for government, defense, critical infrastructure, and regulated industries.
